Got an idea? Before you make it real, first let us bring it to life in Real 3D!

Architectural Pre-Vis

Do you have blueprints for future housing or commercial sites? Or you just have a sketch or even just an idea for a renovation? Well we at Real 3D can help you. We use the latest in visualisation software to model, texture, light and render a near photorealistic shot of your future project. You and your clients will see it in all it's glory well before construction begins, and make any necessary changes if needed saving you time and money.

Product & Packaging

Why slave away for hours sketching down your product ideas when in a moment it could all change. We can visualise it for you and make changes as needed while you wait. Not only that, but we can even print your ideas out in 3D! So you can hold it in your hands and get a real feel for it before it goes to production. Additionally we can visualise any packaging artwork to go with your product to either see or use before it hits the market.

Medical Usage

Did you know 3D technology is being used more and more in the medical industry? That's right, we at Real 3D can help model human blood vessels, prosthetic parts and even assisting researches in printing compounds at the molecular level. We also have extensive experience modelling, visualising and printing medical models for cancerous tumors aiding scientists to research and discover treatments for patients.

1. First we obtain your existing plans or conduct a site measure. From that information we develop a scaled 3d model. You decide the level of detail you require.

2. Next we begin to add texture and detail to your model. Based on your specifications and requirements to the design we will bring your ideas to LIFE!

3. Finally we add reflection and lighting for a more photo-realistic view. We can provide you with detailed dimension plans and elevations in all phases.
